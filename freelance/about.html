ABOUT US

A Few Words About Us

Idrish Enterprises was Founded in 1987 by Mr. Mohammed H Patanwala, to quickly become the Most Trusted Enterprise in Chennai for All Electrical Solutions and Soon Became the Most Recognized Company in Tamil Nadu. Idrish Enterprises Be-lives in Action, Progress and Growth. The Dynamic Company has Achieved many Milestones since Incorporated, But Nothing Keeps Us from Seeking more and more. Since we have come so far we seek to Flourish Our Business in All over India and then Around the Globe. We're Soon to be Recognized All over India. We're Distributors for Cable Ties, Stainless Steel Hose, G.I Metal Hose, PVC Sleeves, Heat Shrink Sleeves, Cooper Lugs, Aluminum Lugs, S.S Ties, Flexible Hoses, Container Seal Ties, Panel Accessories.

Our Clients are always at the center of all we do at IDRISH ENTERPRISES. Our philosophy extends beyond our direct clients as a leading distributor of Electrical Panel Board Accessories. We always start with your ideas and think from the perspective of your end customers, their areas of pain, and design a solution that solves important challenges to aid your business. Idrish Enterprises is entirely focused on these five factors. Effective Synergy, On-Time Delivery, Deep Expertise, Total Ownership & Feedback, Results Oriented.

We will make our company a fantastic place to work by inspiring our employees to constantly innovate and add more value to our customers and the environment, ultimately increasing stakeholder value. Our aim is to develop and strengthen our leadership position in wires and cables, as well as to continue investing in technology to increase operational efficiencies, customer satisfaction, and sales, as well as to strengthen brand recognition.
